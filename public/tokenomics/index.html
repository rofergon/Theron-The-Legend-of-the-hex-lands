Token flow, utility, and sinks

Faucet

Faith (in-game)

Earned by devotees and play. Non-tradable, convertible only through gameplay.

  • Inputs: temples + devotee assignments
  • Outputs: conversion feed to HEX

Utility

HEX (utility)

Minted by burning Faith at a fixed rate. Primary sink for progression.

  • Spends: boosts, building speed-ups, cosmetic slots

Purchased with OCT or minted by burning HEX (high fixed rate). Controls governance gates.

  • Stability: protocol-owned liquidity + buybacks
