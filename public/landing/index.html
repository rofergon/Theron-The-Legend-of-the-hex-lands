How to play (quick loop)

Mark, build, assign, convert. Repeat.

Plan & gather

Paint farm/mine/gather zones; villagers harvest food, wood, and stone automatically.

Build & devote

Place blueprints, haul materials, and assign devotees in temples to generate Faith per hour.

Convert & expand

Convert Faith → Token1, spend it on upgrades/boosts, and expand faster to unlock more buildings.

Tokenomics at a glance

Two tokens, clear roles. Token1 you earn; Token2 you buy with USDC.

In-game Earned

Token1

  • Earned by converting Faith from temple devotees.
  • Spend on upgrades, boosts, and building acceleration.
  • Soft supply; sinks keep the economy in check.
Faith → Token1 → Upgrades/boosts
Premium Buy with USDC

Token2

  • Purchased directly with USDC.
  • Used for lands, starter chests, and premium cosmetics.
  • Can also be minted by burning large amounts of Token1 at a high fixed rate.
  • Keeps value high; no farming path, only purchase.
USDC → Token2 → Lands/Chests/Premium
